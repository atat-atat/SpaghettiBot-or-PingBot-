The experimental Music Bot-addition to PingBot is still unstable and has been categorized in the Test commands section.
To use the experimental Music Bot, you must be connected to a channel where voice can be used.
Music Bot is not always on/available, so these commands may not work at specific times.

Music Bot Options -
play     -- starts a voice connection and begins to stream a song selected by the user. USAGE: !music play <YT_URL>
playlast -- plays the last streamed song.
stop     -- stops the music stream.
resume   -- resumes the music stream.
pause    -- pauses the music stream.
dl       -- same thing as play.
disc     -- disconnects from the voice channel.
